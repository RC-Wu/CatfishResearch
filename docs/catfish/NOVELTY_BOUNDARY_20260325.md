# Catfish Novelty Boundary

Date: 2026-03-25

This is the short version for paper writing. It states what Catfish can claim safely after checking primary sources for The AI Scientist, AI Scientist-v2, AI co-scientist, AlphaEvolve, EvoScientist, and adjacent follow-up work.

## Unsafe Claims

Avoid these:

- Catfish is the first evolving AI scientist.
- Catfish is the first autonomous scientific-agent system with competition.
- Catfish is the first scientific-agent system to use multiple models or providers.
- Catfish is the first system to maintain evolving capability state or memory.
- Catfish is the first system to allocate resources adaptively across branches or candidates.

Those claims are already weakened by prior work from The AI Scientist, AI Scientist-v2, AI co-scientist, AlphaEvolve, EvoScientist, CausalEvolve, and SAGA.

## Safer Claims

These are more defensible:

- Catfish introduces a competitive ecological architecture for scientific-agent teams.
- Catfish makes the unit of selection larger than a single hypothesis or program by co-evaluating provider, model, and agent-group composition.
- Catfish couples capability updates, bargaining, and cross-project scheduling in one hierarchical control layer.
- Catfish studies whether explicit catfish-pressure improves exploration quality and organizational adaptation in evolving scientific-agent populations.

## How To Frame The Story

Use prior work for the basics:

- The AI Scientist and AI Scientist-v2 establish autonomous AI scientist pipelines and structured search over scientific trajectories.
- AI co-scientist establishes multi-agent scientific reasoning with tournament-style refinement.
- AlphaEvolve establishes evaluator-driven LLM evolution with heterogeneous model roles.
- EvoScientist establishes the phrase and concept of an evolving AI scientist.
- CausalEvolve and SAGA weaken claims around updated structured state and adaptive objectives.

Then make the Catfish claim narrower:

> Catfish does not introduce evolution into AI scientist systems per se. Its likely contribution is a particular ecology for evolution: explicit inter-group catfish pressure, joint selection over team composition and model/provider choice, and a scheduler that links resource bargaining with evolving capability state across multiple projects.

| Topic | Claim strength |

| Evolutionary AI scientist in general | Unsafe |

## Minimal Recommended Paper Sentence

> Relative to prior AI scientist and evolutionary discovery systems, Catfish is best understood as a systems-level contribution: it organizes evolving scientific-agent teams through explicit inter-group competition, joint selection over team and model composition, and a hierarchical scheduler that couples capability updates with resource bargaining across concurrent projects.
